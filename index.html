Velour

A Premium Discord Fan Shop

A private boutique bot for creators who want to offer exclusive, intimate content to their most devoted fans — powered by a custom Seals economy.

Custom Economy Waitlist System Fan Leaderboard Drop Countdowns Order Management

What it does

Everything a creator
actually needs

🛍️
Boutique Shop

List exclusive items — physical, digital, or personal. Each with stock tracking, category tags, and custom emotes. Add, hide, and restock items any time.

Seals Economy

Fans earn Seals through daily logins, engagement tasks, and gifts from Saph. Spend them in the shop. Every transaction is logged with a full ledger.

🎟️
Waitlist System

Sold-out items get a waitlist queue. Fans hold their spot with 2 Seals — fully refundable if they leave. Auto-DM everyone when you restock.

⏳
Drop Countdowns

Schedule drops with a live countdown that edits itself every minute in your #drops channel. When time hits zero, the item auto-restocks and waitlisted fans are DM'd.

📋
Order Management

Every purchase posts to a private #requests channel with Complete, Invalid, and Refund buttons. Staff can add notes and buyers are DM'd when their order is actioned.

👑
Fan Leaderboard

Top 10 fans ranked by combined score. Five tiers with Discord role assignment, badge displays, and one-time Seals bonuses when fans level up.

Command reference

Everything at
your fingertips

Shopping
/shopBrowse all available items with stock levels
/item <id>View item details with buy or waitlist buttons
/buy <id>Purchase an item with confirmation flow
/purchasesSee everything you've bought
Seals & Earning
/balanceCheck your Seals balance and recent activity
/dailyClaim your 3 Seals daily bonus
/earnSee all ways to earn Seals
/task complete <id>Complete an engagement task
/gift @userSend Seals to another fan (100/day limit)
Waitlists
/waitlist join <id>Hold a spot for a sold-out item
/waitlist leave <id>Leave and get your 2 Seals refunded
/waitlist viewSee all your current waitlist positions
Gifts & Leaderboard
/gifts viewSee unclaimed gifts from Saph
